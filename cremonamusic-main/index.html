Our Story我們的故事

About Cremona關於克雷莫納

With our commitment and dedication to bring out the best in our students whilst providing a nurturing and holistic musical experience, we welcome you to Cremona Music of Strings, a Premier School where Music Comes Alive!我們致力於培育音樂教養及提供完整音樂體驗，引導出學生最佳的潛力。歡迎您來到讓音樂充滿活力的優質學校－－Cremona Music of Strings！

Cremona Music of Strings 台北音樂工作室 is the first professional strings school in Malaysia with a music shop within its premises. In Malaysia 2010, it expanded to include a Violin and Viola department. Since then, it has continued to grow in terms of student population and teachers. Cremona Music Shop is located within Cremona Music of Strings’s premises. The shop retails quality stringed instruments, bows and accessories. Instruments service (repairing & restoring) and maintenance. All products are imported directly from Taiwan Handmade factory which supplies to Australia, Europe and the States. 台北音樂工作室（Cremona Music of Strings）是馬來西亞第一個設有樂器商店的專業弦樂學校。 2010年，工作室在馬來西亞增設了小提琴和中提琴系。此後，學生人數和教師隊伍持續成長。台北音樂工作室內的樂器店（Cremona Music Shop）販售高品質的弦樂器、琴弓及配件，並提供樂器維修保養服務。所有產品均直接從台灣手工樂器工廠進口，該工廠也向澳洲、歐洲和美國供貨。
